About ACE

ACE (American College Education) Institute is a revolutionary concept of higher education that has never been implemented in Pakistan. In collaboration with Community Colleges from California USA, ACE is bringing live synchronous teaching and assessment for undergraduate students to complete the first two years of their four-year Bachelor’s degree in Pakistan in the fields such as Data Analytics, Cyber Security, and Paralegal studies.

Success Stories


I had an enriching two-years of synchronous learning experience. The instructors gave spectacular live lectures delivered via web – on Canvas delivery platform, lectures which helped me navigat e my personal learning journey. My AST degree has allowed me to transfer to Cal State Fullerton where I just finished my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. I met some wonderful people and have them as friends for life. “The two-year associate degree transfer program was definitely a place where I thrived as a student and a person”

When schools and colleges began locking down due to Covid-19 all of us felt very uncertain about our academic progress. It was such a relief to find that Santiago Canyon College (SCC) adapted full online instructions, swiftly and competently. Thanks to SCC’s online platform, I was able to complete my AS degree in Physics and was accepted into UC Berkeley’s Mechanical Engineering Program.

My experience with Associate of Science Transfer (AST) program during the covid time we went to 100% asynchronous program. All of our lectures were live via web.
